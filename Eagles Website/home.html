About Us

Welcome to our cozy corner at the Steeleville Eagles! Situated in the heart of Steeleville since 1982, we're your go-to spot for cold drinks, good company, and a laid-back atmosphere. Swing by for a casual hangout with friends, catch up on the latest gossip, or simply unwind after a long day. We're proud to be a part of this community, and we're here to make sure you feel right at home. Cheers!

F.O.E. Mission Statement
"The Fraternal Order of Eagles is an international non-profit organization uniting fraternally in the spirit of liberty, truth, justice, and equality, to make human life more desirable by lessening its ills and promoting peace, prosperity, gladness and hope."
